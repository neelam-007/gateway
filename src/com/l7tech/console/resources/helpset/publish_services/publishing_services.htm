Publishing Services

There are two methods available to publish a service in the Manager: the Publish Service wizard or manual publishing. The wizard guides users through the process with a series of prompts. Advanced administrators who use the WSDL creator must manually create and enable their own policies.

Publish Service Wizard

The Publish Service wizard guides a user through locating the service, selecting the service credentials, and adding users to the policy. The wizard simplifies the service creation process for people who are new to policy creation.

Launching the Publish Service wizard

There are three ways to launch the Publish Service wizard:

  • In the Home section, select the Publish protected Web service link.

  • Under the Action menu, select Publish service.

  • In the Services section, right-click the main service. Select the Publish Service option that appears.
