Creating Policies

overview

Policy Model

Policies in the Manager are built using the established policy model. Each line, or assertion, in a policy must follow this model's rules or the policy will not validate as "OK." Every policy begins with the assertion logic folder. This folder establishes the first level of rules in the policy. The second level of the policy includes the security assertions in the appropriate order. The third level of the policy includes additional assertion logic folders and their associated users/groups.

Policy Levels

The assertions available in the Manager each have a logic level within a policy. These levels are reflected in the table below.

 

Policy Level

Assertion

1

Assertion logic folder (i.e. all/at least one assertion must evaluate to true)

2

2

2

3

2
