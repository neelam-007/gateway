Technical Support

Layer 7 Technologies is committed to providing customers with quality technical support for the SecureSpan™ Solution. Support is provided in three optional service tiers:

Tier 1

Non-critical email support: support@layer7tech.com

A Technical Support Representative will respond within 24 hours.

Tier 2

Toll free telephone support: 1-800-681-9377, ext. 209

A Technical Support Representative will call back within four hours.

Tier 3

Dedicated personal support: A Layer 7 Technologies Support Engineer will be assigned to your company with a guaranteed call back time of 15 minutes.

 

For more information about the support tiers available with the SecureSpan™ Solution, please call 1-800-681-9377.

Requesting Support

Before requesting support, please gather the following information:

  • Company name

  • Contact person and information (email and phone number)

  • Location, nature, and details of problem.
