At Layer 7 Technologies, our commitment to exceptional service culminates in the advanced level of technical support that we provide for the SecureSpan™ products. Whether by email or phone, our knowledgeable Technical Support staff quickly respond to support requests, providing information and solutions that minimize wasted time, or escalating requests when required to ensure the speedy resolution of more complex problems.

 

Support is provided in three service tiers:

Tier 1

Non-critical email support: support@layer7tech.com

A Technical Support Representative will respond within 24 hours.

Tier 2

Toll free telephone support: 1-800-681-9377, ext. 209

A Technical Support Representative will call back within four hours.

Tier 3

Dedicated personal support: A Layer 7 Technologies Support Engineer will be assigned to your company with a guaranteed call back time of 15 minutes.

 

For more information about the support tiers available with SecureSpan™, email support@layer7tech.com, visit www.layer7tech.com, or call 1-800-681-9377.

Requesting Support

Before requesting support, please gather the following information:

  • Company name

  • Contact person and information (email and phone number)

  • Location, nature, and details of problem.
